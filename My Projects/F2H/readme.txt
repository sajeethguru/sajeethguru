F2H is a Cloud App developement project with whick a local farmer can directly sell his products to hostel messes through this app.
This Project was developed using IBM Cloud,Python Flask HTML,CSS and Javascript.
farmer can login to this app, upload his products with price and available quantity and view for orders.
hostel can login shop products ,rate products, track order and view their order history.
both farmer and hostel were monitered by admin, they could login after registering only after verified by admin
